    We created a Python script to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. To accomplish this, we utilized a a python library(citypy), the OpenWeather API, and a little common sense to create a representative model of weather across world cities.

    Our objective was to build a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships:

    * Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
    * Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
    * Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
    * Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
